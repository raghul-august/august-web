# Understanding the Information You See in August

August AI is designed to help you make sense of your health by simplifying complex information like lab reports, prescriptions, symptoms, and nutrition data. 

## Important Notes

The suggestions, summaries & calculations you see are based on widely accepted clinical and scientific knowledge. However, they're generated by an AI which can contain mistakes or errors. You should explore and verify the information further.
